Abilities

Passive traits, daily powers, enchantments, feats, and conditional triggers accumulated across a lifetime of adventuring.

Passive

Polymath

Gain temporary proficiency in any unfamiliar subject. Side effect: starts 3 new projects per month.

Pattern Sight

Reveal hidden connections between unrelated systems. Always active. Cannot be dispelled.

Perpetual Motion

Rest actions convert into creative output. Long rests take twice as long. Cannot idle.

Monkey See, Monkey Do

Absorb proficiencies from nearby allies. Duration scales with exposure.

Tradition Breaker

Advantage on all checks against established methods.

Daily

Bardic Inspiration

Grant target ally one inspiration die. Listeners make WIS save or be moved. Recharges at dawn.

Mana Restoration

Withdraw to recharge. Restore all spell slots. +2 WIS until next long rest.

Chromatic Mastery

Improvise in any key. No sheet music required. +2 PER until next long rest.

Worldbuilding

Enchant any conversation. Target “what if” spirals into a fully realized alternate reality.

Enchantment

Warmth of the Stranger

Remove “outsider” debuff from newcomers. Permanent aura, 30ft radius.

Mentor’s Mark

Buff early-career allies with +2 confidence and direction. Effect persists indefinitely. 15+ targets affected.

Bard’s Friend

Enchant ally as arcane conduit. Target may cast Bardic Inspiration on your behalf. No limit on active conduits.

Feat

Fancy Footwork

On failure, rise with a better version. Redirect all momentum into a new venture. Immunity to discouragement. Cannot be knocked prone.

Rally

AOE inspiration die to all allies in range. Ignores time zones, language barriers, and institutional silos. Once per season.

Conference Architect

Plan and execute a gathering of 600+. Advantage on all logistics checks. Once per year.

Migration

Relocate your life to a new state or country. Massive logistics check. Immune to homesickness.

Ship It

Launch a product, paper, or platform from zero to public. Once per quarter max.

Conditional

Scope Creep

Trigger: planning any project. Scope expands by two domains. Cannot be suppressed.

Edge of Chaos

Trigger: pressure or ambiguity. All stats +1. Mundane routine: all stats -1.

Spark of Curiosity

Trigger: unfamiliar idea. Summon a new collaboration before the conversation ends.

Science Communicator

Trigger: jargon detected. Remove “jargon” debuff from any audience. Works at ComiCon.
